What is the Inferni Imperium?

The Inferni Imperium is an empire in Nota and is led by an Empress named, Kailyn Hadroni

It is considered a totalarian monarchy and is quite frequently known as the greates nation. Its capital is the Grand City of Elysium and it is often reffered to as the capital of the world.

Although it is quite diverse, its official religion is The Path of the Flame. Some religions such as Martism have been denounced.
